Gzashi-Travel App

Role & Context

UI/UX Designer

Individual Project

Duration: 1 months

Figma Link

Project Overview

"Gzashi" is an all-in-one travel companion app that simplifies trip planning and documentation. Users can create personalized itineraries, access real-time weather updates, discover locations, and receive tailored travel suggestions. The app features an intuitive interface for managing trips, viewing saved destinations, and documenting travel experiences through photos and notes.

Problem

Today's travelers struggle with managing multiple apps for different aspects of their journey - weather apps, mapping services, planning tools, and photo storage. This fragmentation creates a disjointed experience where crucial information is scattered across various platforms, making it difficult to access necessary details quickly.

User Persona

How the Persona Was Created

1

I thought about the target users for my project and imagined a typical traveler who values convenience and technology in their travel planning

2

I read travel blogs, user reviews of travel apps, and forum posts to understand common challenges and preferences of travelers.

3

Based on what I learned, I identified important traits, goals, and pain points that a user like Sarah might have.

4

Using these insights, I created Sarah Turner, a fictional character who represents my target audience. Her background, goals, and behaviors reflect the kind of user I want to design for.
